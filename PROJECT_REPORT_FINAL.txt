# PROJECT REPORT: PHARMASYNC
## SMART PHARMACY INVENTORY & EXPIRY MANAGEMENT SYSTEM
**Date:** June 7, 2026
**Status:** Project Fully Completed

---

### ABSTRACT
PharmaSync is a localized, intelligent inventory management solution designed to optimize pharmacy operations. The system addresses the critical challenges of medicinal stock management, including batch-wise tracking, expiry-sensitive waste reduction, and real-time tax-compliant billing. Built using Django (Backend) and React 19 (Frontend), PharmaSync implements advanced algorithms such as First-In-First-Out (FIFO) stock deduction and automated multi-tier expiry alerts. The system ensures data integrity, reduces manual error, and provides actionable analytics for stock optimization.

---

### 1. PROBLEM STATEMENT
Manual management in local pharmacies leads to:
1. Significant financial loss due to expired stock.
2. Inefficient tracking of batch numbers and unique MRPs.
3. Errors in GST calculations and manual billing.
4. Difficulty in finding alternative medicines (substitutes) when stock is low.
5. Inability to analyze sales trends for better procurement.

---

### 2. LITERATURE SURVEY
Research shows that medicinal products require a specialized inventory approach due to their legal and safety constraints (expiry dates, batch numbers). Standard retail POS systems often fail to provide the granularity needed for pharmaceutical returns and batch-wise FIFO. Research into OCR and Barcode integration highlights the potential for near-zero error rates in medicinal data entry.

---

### 3. RESEARCH GAP
PharmaSync addresses the gap between expensive, complex enterprise hospital systems and basic billing software. It provides a unique "Smart-Match" substitute engine and automated return-eligibility flagging—features typically missing in small-scale local solutions.

---

### 4. OBJECTIVES
- To track inventory at a granular batch level.
- To implement automated expiry monitoring and alerting.
- To ensure oldest stock is sold first via FIFO logic.
- To provide professional tax-invoicing and customer history tracking.
- To visualize stock health and sales performance through analytics.

---

### 5. METHODOLOGY (OBJECTIVE-WISE)
- **Objective 1 (Batch Tracking):** Developed a relational database model in PostgreSQL where each `Medicine` is linked to multiple `Batch` entries. This allows the system to store unique dates and prices for the same product.
- **Objective 2 (Hybrid Entry):** Implemented a flexible inventory form that supports manual input, auto-suggestions, and is architected to support OCR and Barcode scanning.
- **Objective 3 (Expiry Alerts):** Created a date-difference algorithm that runs in real-time, categorizing stock into 'Critical' (<30 days), 'Warning' (<90 days), and 'Safe' levels.
- **Objective 4 (FIFO Billing):** Developed a backend logic in Django that automatically sorts and deducts quantity from the earliest expiring batch during a sale transaction.
- **Objective 5 (Return Management):** Created a dedicated logic to flag batches that are within the distributor return window (90-180 days before expiry).
- **Objective 6 (Analytics):** Integrated Recharts in the frontend to visualize sales trends, fast-moving items, and dead stock identified via Django aggregation queries.

---

### 6. RESULT DISCUSSION
- **Functional Validation:** The system successfully prevents negative stock and ensures that only valid, non-expired batches are available for sale.
- **Data Consistency:** Implemented strict batch validation that prevents adding inconsistent data (e.g., same batch number but different expiry dates).
- **Operational Efficiency:** Billing process is now instant, with automated GST calculation and inventory reconciliation.
- **User Experience:** The system features a modern "Glassmorphism" UI with dark-mode support, ensuring a professional and responsive experience across devices.
- **Reporting:** Successfully implemented a CSV export feature for detailed analytics reports, enabling offline auditing and management analysis.

---

### 7. TECHNICAL STACK
- **Backend:** Django 5.0, Django REST Framework, PostgreSQL.
- **Frontend:** React 19 (TypeScript), Vite, Tailwind CSS 4.0.
- **Security:** JWT Authentication, Strict Relational Constraints.
- **Design:** Lucide-React Icons, Recharts (Data Visualization).

---

### 8. CONCLUSION
PharmaSync has evolved into a robust, professional-grade pharmacy management tool. It successfully automates the critical lifecycle of medicinal products, from procurement and batch-tracking to smart billing and analytics, significantly reducing the risks and inefficiencies of manual pharmacy management.
